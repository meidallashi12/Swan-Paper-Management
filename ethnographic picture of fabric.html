Ethnographic Pain Points & Tensions Observed
Z-02 · Production
Unplanned Downtime Visibility Gap
Shift supervisors rely on verbal handover; no real-time OEE alert triggers cross-zone notifications to maintenance.
Z-01 · Procurement
Safety Stock Drift
Manual reorder point management leads to over-stocking of C-class items and stock-outs of critical A-class fiber grades.
Z-03 · Quality
CAPA Closure Delay
NCRs raised at production level are not systematically escalated; CAPA closure rates fall below the 90% monthly target.
Z-04 · Maintenance
Spare Parts Data Fragmentation
Spare parts catalog exists in both CMMS and spreadsheet; discrepancies cause delays in breakdown response procurement.
Z-05 · Finance
Cost-per-Tonne Latency
Daily cost flash depends on manual entry from three systems; data often arrives the following morning, reducing its operational value.
Z-06 · HR / HSE
Skills Matrix Staleness
Competency records are updated quarterly but skills gaps surface in real time during shift handover, creating unquantified risk.
