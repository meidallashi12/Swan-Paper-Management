# Stakeholder Analysis — Swan Paper Fabrics

| Stakeholder | Role & Responsibility | Importance | Influence | Interests | Concerns |
|---|---|:---:|:---:|---|---|
| Founder / Owner | Runs the business end-to-end — oversees finances, makes purchasing and hiring decisions, sets direction, and handles key business relationships | ![High](https://img.shields.io/badge/High-1a7f37?style=flat-square) | ![High](https://img.shields.io/badge/High-1a7f37?style=flat-square) | Business growth, healthy margins, smooth daily operations, reliable supply and delivery | Cash flow gaps, machine breakdowns, unreliable suppliers or drivers |
| Machine Operator | Operates and monitors production machinery, ensures the lines run correctly, and flags any mechanical issues before they cause downtime | ![High](https://img.shields.io/badge/High-1a7f37?style=flat-square) | ![Medium](https://img.shields.io/badge/Medium-b36800?style=flat-square) | Machines in good condition, clear production targets, manageable workload | Equipment failures mid-shift, lack of spare parts, pressure to maintain output with no backup |
| Packaging Worker | Receives finished product off the line and prepares it for dispatch — bagging, labeling, and organizing by order or product type | ![High](https://img.shields.io/badge/High-1a7f37?style=flat-square) | ![Low](https://img.shields.io/badge/Low-57606a?style=flat-square) | Steady work pace, clear instructions on packaging per order, organized storage area | Bottlenecks from overproduction, inconsistent product output, unclear order priorities |
| Driver | Handles business-to-business deliveries to retail stores and direct clients, manages loading and ensures orders arrive correctly | ![High](https://img.shields.io/badge/High-1a7f37?style=flat-square) | ![Low](https://img.shields.io/badge/Low-57606a?style=flat-square) | Accurate manifests, reliable vehicle, clear delivery schedule | Late packaging, vehicle maintenance issues, unclear drop-off instructions |
| Raw Material Supplier | Delivers tissue rolls and other input materials needed for production on a recurring basis | ![High](https://img.shields.io/badge/High-1a7f37?style=flat-square) | ![Medium](https://img.shields.io/badge/Medium-b36800?style=flat-square) | Consistent orders, timely payment, predictable demand | Last-minute order changes, payment delays, low order volumes |
| Retail Store Partners | The two stores that stock and sell Swan Paper products directly to end consumers | ![High](https://img.shields.io/badge/High-1a7f37?style=flat-square) | ![Medium](https://img.shields.io/badge/Medium-b36800?style=flat-square) | Reliable restocking, consistent product quality, fair pricing | Delays in delivery, packaging inconsistencies, stock shortages |
| B2B Clients | Businesses that purchase in bulk directly from the fabric for internal or resale use | ![High](https://img.shields.io/badge/High-1a7f37?style=flat-square) | ![Medium](https://img.shields.io/badge/Medium-b36800?style=flat-square) | On-time delivery, competitive pricing, order flexibility | Quality variations, unfulfilled orders, limited communication on delays |
